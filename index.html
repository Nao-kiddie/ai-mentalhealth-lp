FAQ

Questions

What kinds of organizations do you serve?

Companies of all sizes working on employee mental health, senior-care facilities and providers, and domestic or international companies offering AI services in the mental-health field.

We are an overseas company — can you support our Japan market entry?

Yes. We provide end-to-end support for entering the Japanese market: localization, medical supervision, regulatory guidance, and partner and client development. Consultations are available in English.

Can we engage you for consulting only?

Yes. Product deployment is not a prerequisite — program diagnosis, KPI design, PoC support and medical supervision of AI services are all available as standalone engagements.

Does the AI make medical diagnoses?

No. Our services do not perform medical diagnosis. Multimodal analysis informed by expert knowledge aims to detect early signs of change in mental wellbeing. Details are explained at deployment.

How is personal data handled?

Conversations are encrypted and never provided to employers or facilities in personally identifiable form. Only statistically analyzed, organization-level trends are shared.
